General Pediatrics

The Pediatrician is a physician of the children. He gives medical care to kids who are suffering from any health issue. A Pediatrician also controls the mental and
     physical conditions of the children. He diagnoses and treats many disorders in children involving infections, injuries, cancers, organ infections, cancers, Genetic
     and congenital problems. Pediatricians are also included in the early detection, prevention, and management of issues, i.e., Behavioral problems, functional disabilities,
     social tensions, mental diseases involving stress, and depression.

Treatment For Diseases In Pregnancy

The prevalence of medical problems in pregnancy is increasing because of a complex interplay between demographic and lifestyle factors, and developments in modern
     medicine. Women are delaying childbearing until later in life. Older women are more likely to have acquired medical disorders, such as hypertension and obesity; they
     are also at higher risk of gestational diabetes and venous thromboembolism. Medical and surgical advances have enabled women to become pregnant despite having
     chronic conditions that would previously have precluded pregnancy.
